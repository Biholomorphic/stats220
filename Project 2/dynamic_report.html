Data Collected via Energy Drink Observations

This analysis is based on observational data collected across University of Auckland and AUT campuses, documenting the presence and quantities of energy drinks in various campus locations. The data captures snapshots of individual brand quantities observed at specific times and locations, enabling insights into campus consumption patterns and brand prevalence.

Summary of Observations

Across 61 observations, we documented energy drink instances ranging from a minimum of 1 to a maximum of 4 drinks per location snapshot. The mean observed quantity was 1.54 drinks, with a median of 1, indicating that while most observations show relatively low quantities, some locations captured clusters of drinks.
